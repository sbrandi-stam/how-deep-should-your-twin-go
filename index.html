      Declared evidence gaps

      No controlled same-building comparison quantifies MPC benefit across TABS, radiators and all-air systems: the emission-system modifiers rest on juxtaposition of separate studies.

      Thermal-storage evidence comes from residential water heaters and DHW tank tests, not central plant buffers.

      Simulation studies (R2, R18) favor depth; a real test building (R4) decouples prediction accuracy from control performance. Presented here as context dependence, not a resolved contradiction.

      Battery evidence (R6) comes from market-participation studies rather than building energy management.
